Research & Innovation at Shivalik College of Engineering

Link: https://shivalikcollege.edu.in/research/

Shivalik College of Engineering, Dehradun, is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation through a dynamic research ecosystem that spans multiple disciplines. Our research initiatives empower both students and faculty to engage in hands-on projects, scientific experiments, and industry-relevant problem-solving. 

By promoting collaborations and encouraging undergraduate participation, Shivalik College of Engineering transforms ideas into impactful solutions addressing the challenges of the 21st century. The research ecosystem further nurtures future leaders, equipping them to develop innovative technologies that benefit society. Supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and research grants, Shivalik College of Engineering is dedicated to creating a vibrant environment where creativity, inquiry, and excellence drive progress.
Our Vision
To facilitate transformative discoveries, drive excellence through innovation, and generate knowledge that uplifts society.
Our Mission
M1: Forge strong alliances between researchers, industry, and government to maximize impact.
M2: Promote ethical research practices and a culture of shared responsibility.
M3: Empower researchers to secure and manage multidisciplinary grants.
M4: Encourage community engagement and support entrepreneurship through our Innovation HUB @ Shivalik.
