Frequently Asked Questions

What are the facilities provided by the college during the complete program?

Shivalik College of Engineering provides a variety of facilities to students during their B.Tech program. Some of these may include:

Classrooms: Modern and well-equipped classrooms with necessary audio-visual aids for effective teaching and learning.

Laboratories: Well-equipped and well-maintained laboratories for students to conduct experiments and practicals.

Library: A well-stocked library with a wide range of books, journals, and e-resources.

Computing facilities: Access to high-speed internet and modern computing facilities, including computers and software.

Hostel facilities: Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with basic amenities such as furniture, bedding, and 24/7 security.

Sports facilities: Access to sports facilities such as a gym, sports fields, and courts for various sports.

Medical facilities: Access to on-campus medical facilities, including a health center, ambulance service and doctor on call.

Extra-curricular activities: Opportunities for students to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities such as clubs, societies, and cultural events.

Career counseling and placement services: Help and guidance to students in finding internships, jobs and career opportunities.
