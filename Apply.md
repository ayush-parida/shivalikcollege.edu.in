Instructions

The online application is for admission to programs offered by Shivalik College of Engineering and College of Pharmacy.
The application form fee is Rs. 1000/- and is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Email IDs submitted at the time of registration will be used for all official correspondence until the enrolment process is completed. Any request for change in email ID will not be permitted.
Applicants must ensure that all information provided in the application form is accurate and complete. Submission of incorrect or misleading information may result in rejection of the application.
Applicants are advised to carefully check the eligibility criteria for the chosen program before applying.
Applicants are advised to retain the payment receipt or transaction ID for future reference.
Important updates related to admission, counselling, or document verification will be communicated via the registered email ID and/or mobile number. Applicants should regularly check their email, including the spam/junk folder.
For a quick response to queries, applicants are strongly advised to use the ‘Any Queries? Ask Us’ option available on the dashboard after starting the application.
Submission of the application form does not guarantee admission. Admission is subject to eligibility verification, document validation, and compliance with institutional norms.
By submitting the application, the applicant agrees to abide by the rules and regulations of the institution.

Eligibility Criteria
B.Tech Courses
10+2 with minimum 45% Marks in Physics, Mathematics along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Computer Science as one of the subjects.
B.Tech (Lateral entry)
Passed Diploma examination with at least 50% marks in any branch of Engineering and Technology.
Passed B.Sc. Degree from a recognized University as defined by UGC, with at least 50% marks and passed 10+2 examination with Mathematics as a subject.
Provided that the students belong to B.Sc. Stream, shall clear the subjects Engineering Graphics/ Engineering Drawing and Engineering Mechanics of the First Year Engineering Programme along with the Second-year subjects.
Provided that the students belong to B.Sc. Stream shall be considered only after filling the supernumerary seats in this category with students belonging to the Diploma stream.
Passed D. Voc. Stream in the same or allied sector.
In the above cases, a suitable bridge course, if required such as in Mathematics may be conducted.
